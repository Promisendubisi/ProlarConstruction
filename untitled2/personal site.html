PROLAR CONSTRUCTION

Prolar construction is a subsidiary of prolar consult and is a leading construction company in West Africa with it's head office situated in Kano state, Nigeria.

The company started off as just an idea which came to the CEO as far back as a decade ago when he was house hunting during his NYSC service year in the stae of Kano.

List of Services Rendered at Prolar Construction

  • Construction of office space
  • Sales of landed properties in strategic and economical locations
  • Consultation services on contruction and real estate projects
